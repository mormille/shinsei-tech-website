About Us

Shinsei Technology is a technology-driven company focused on delivering scalable, data-driven, autonomous solutions for precision agriculture. Our goal is to bring agricultural production to a new age by translating advanced scientific research into reliable, fully-integrated autonomous tools that support decision-making in real agricultural environments.

Our Vision

At Shinsei Technology, we envision a future where farmers can produce more with fewer resources. By combining autonomous drones and artificial intelligence, we aim to reduce production costs, increase productivity, and enable smarter, data-driven decisions across large-scale agriculture.

Innovation

Driving the next wave of agricultural innovation through autonomous drones, edge computing, and artificial intelligence.

Autonomy

Fully autonomous flights and real-time AI-driven analysis that reduce human intervention, lower operational costs, and enable consistent monitoring across large agricultural areas.

Sustainability

Reducing unnecessary inputs and improving resource efficiency helps farmers lower costs while minimizing environmental impact.

Origins

Shinsei Technology originated from applied research activities conducted at Soka University, Japan, within an academic environment strongly focused on artificial intelligence, computer vision, remote sensing, and data-driven systems. Furthermore, Soka University is widely recognized, both in Japan and internationally, for its contributions to robotics research and education.

This academic foundation shaped the company’s approach from its inception. Rather than pursuing experimental or speculative technologies, Shinsei Technology builds upon peer-reviewed state-of-the-art research and well-established methods. The transition from university research to a dedicated company reflects a deliberate focus on execution, scalability, and real-world deployment.

Our Team

Our team is composed of professionals passionate about agriculture, AI, and technology, led by seasoned experts with extensive international experience across Japan, the UK, Brazil, Ghana, and Ethiopia.
